Introduction to Kitten Fostering

Fostering kittens is a rewarding experience that provides young cats with the care and attention they need to grow healthy and strong.

Care Essentials

1. Warmth and Comfort

Kittens cannot regulate their body temperature well. Provide a warm, cozy space with blankets or a heating pad set to low.

2. Litter Training

Use a shallow litter box and unscented litter. Gently place the kitten in the box after meals to encourage use.

Feeding Tips

1. Bottle Feeding

For very young kittens, use kitten-specific formula and a bottle. Never feed cow's milk, as it can cause digestive issues.

2. Transitioning to Solid Food

Gradually introduce wet kitten food mixed with formula at around 4 weeks of age. Monitor their eating habits closely.

Did You Know?

Kittens need to be weighed daily to ensure they're growing. A digital scale can help you track their progress.
